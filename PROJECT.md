# Relume Prompt for Agenture.fr

> **Important:** Content updates only - no style modifications to the Webflow template.

## Company Description

Agenture is a cutting-edge AI development agency based in Paris 8ème, France, specializing in building custom AI agents and implementing intelligent automation solutions that transform business operations. We help companies automate complex workflows, optimize business processes, and leverage the power of autonomous AI systems to gain competitive advantages. Our expertise spans custom AI agent development, workflow automation, process optimization, and seamless integration of AI into existing business infrastructure. We serve mid-size to enterprise clients across France and Europe who want to embrace the agentic AI revolution.

## Goals

**Primary Goal:** Generate qualified leads and demo requests from businesses seeking AI automation solutions

**Secondary Goals:**
- Showcase our AI agent development expertise and technical capabilities
- Build trust and credibility as a new, innovative Paris-based AI agency
- Educate visitors about the potential of AI agents and automation
- Encourage visitors to book a discovery call or consultation

## Other Relevant Notes

### Design Direction
- Modern, clean 2025 aesthetic with bold typography and generous whitespace
- Dark mode-first design with subtle gradients and glow effects (AI/tech aesthetic)
- Micro-interactions and subtle animations to demonstrate technical sophistication
- Use dynamic visual elements that suggest intelligence and automation
- Avoid generic stock photos; prefer abstract 3D elements, particle effects, or custom illustrations
- French language for all copy

### Pages to Include

**Homepage**
- Hero section with compelling value proposition about AI agents transforming business
- Brief overview of core services (AI Agents, Automation, Integration)
- Social proof section with client logos or trust indicators
- Featured case study or success metric highlight
- How it works / our process section (3-4 steps)
- Call-to-action for booking a consultation

**Services Page**
- Overview of all service offerings
- Individual service blocks for:
  - Custom AI Agent Development
  - Workflow Automation
  - Business Process Optimization
  - AI Integration & Consulting
- Each service should have clear benefits and use cases

**Solutions / Use Cases Page**
- Industry-specific or department-specific solutions
- Examples: Sales Automation, Customer Support AI, Operations Optimization
- Each with brief description of the problem solved

**About / L'Agence Page**
- Company story and mission
- Why we started Agenture
- Our approach to AI development
- Location in Paris 8ème (6 rue de Naples)
- Team section (if applicable) or founder profile

**Case Studies / Réalisations Page**
- Portfolio of AI agent projects
- Results and metrics achieved
- Client testimonials

**Blog / Insights Page**
- Articles about AI agents, automation trends, and best practices
- Thought leadership content

**Contact Page**
- Contact form for inquiries
- Option to book a discovery call (Calendly integration)
- Office location in Paris 8ème
- Email and phone contact

### Section Guidelines
- Keep pages between 5-8 sections for optimal length
- Include clear CTAs at the end of each major page
- Use FAQ sections where appropriate to address common questions
- Include trust signals (certifications, partnerships, client logos)

### Specific Components to Consider
- Animated hero with gradient background or subtle particle effect
- Pricing tiers or engagement models section
- Process/timeline visualization
- Before/after or comparison sections for automation benefits
- Stats/metrics highlighting AI impact (time saved, efficiency gains)
- Newsletter signup for AI insights

### Brand Voice
- Professional yet approachable
- Technical expertise without jargon
- Forward-thinking and innovative
- Confident but not arrogant
- French language, formal "vous" form
